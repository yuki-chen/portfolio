PROJECTS OVERVIEW .

BACKGROUND .   The Italian Garden Project is a nonprofit organization aimed at preserving and documenting Italian customs in the realms of old world food and gardening tradition. It is our goal to showcase the living heirlooms through a mobile & tablet application on the user’s personal device by enabling them to follow a virtually guided walk-in tour at the said garden.

EXISTING KNOWLEDGE .   The Italian Garden Project’s current website which has a single page dedicated to the Legacy Fig Tree Collection which encompasses their grant proposal. The page has a description of the Legacy Fig Tree Collection that includes information about the history and importance of fig trees as well as the origin and goal of the project.   • Donations

USER TESTING .   All meetings were conducted via Zoom using the Figma Prototype
  • Task 1 (simple): You are interested in viewing the different trees that are at the Italian Garden Project. How would you go about achieving this?
  • Task 2 (simple): You are interested in donating to the Italian Garden Project. How would you go about achieving this?
  • Task 3 (simple): You come across a specific tree on your tour that you’re interested in learning more about. How would you find the information about this tree?
  • Task 4 (complex): Now you are looking to buy a sapling from the Italian Fig Tree Legacy Garden. How would you go about achieving this? Please proceed as if you are actually going to purchase a sapling all the way to checkout.
  • Task 5 (simple): You just purchased a sapling but you are unsure about how to preserve and attend to it. How would you approach this problem?
TAKEAWAYS .   There were no major critical issues or concerns with the prototype, although misunderstandings about certain features on the app did arise. We may need to think more about how we can better help older populations or those who are not technologically proficient navigate the app and understand the app’s purpose.
  • 75% of all users completed Task 1 successfully
  • 100% of all users completed Task 2 successfully
  • 100% of all users completed Task 3 successfully
  • 100% of all users completed Task 4 successfully
  • 75% of all users completed Task 5 successfully
  • Task 1 took on average 2-3 clicks
  • Task 2 took on average 2-3 clicks
  • Task 3 took on average 1-2 clicks
  • Task 4 took on average 4 clicks
  • Task 5 took on average 2 clicks
COLOR SCHEME .   To stay consistent with the Italian Garden Project’s existing website, we will use the same color scheme that the website uses. The existing website uses a monochromatic green color scheme that helps tie all of the different elements together while also creating contrast between sections when needed.
FONTS .   For the same reason, the fonts selected for the app are the same as the current website. Our group made the collaborative decision to change the heading font style to a more legible font.
  • American Scribe
  • Baskerville
  • BD Engraved Regular
